Robo-advisor is a quantitative analysis script written in Python that generates the least volatile portfolio given a list of stocks, with the goal of a 0% return. Includes a 500,000 iteration Monte Carlo simulation that runs in as little as 15 seconds (threading) and generates on average a portfolio with over 40% less volitility compared to the market benchmark.
